Executive Overview

This proposal outlines the design, development, and deployment of a cutting-edge Next-Generation Auto Parts Storefront for PAM Auto Components. By decoupling the frontend (React Router / Hydrogen) from the backend (Shopify) and integrating a powerful headless CMS (Sanity), we will deliver an ultra-fast, highly customizable, and premium shopping experience.

The Opportunity

Traditional e-commerce platforms restrict design flexibility and site performance. PAM Auto Components needs a platform that handles vast auto parts catalogs, precise compatibility filtering, and seamless custom payment gateways (PhonePe) without compromising on speed or user experience.

Our Approach

A headless architecture utilizing Shopify for bulletproof inventory and backend operations, paired with a custom React frontend deployed on Shopify Oxygen. We will empower your editorial team with Sanity CMS for drag-and-drop banner updates, and integrate an AI Chatbot for 24/7 technical part support.

Platform Modules

Customer Storefront Shopify Hydrogen (React)
Core UI: Lightning-fast product browsing, advanced fitment selector, dynamic catalog grids, and premium PAM Auto Components styling.
Commerce: Secure checkout integration with PhonePe, customer accounts, and real-time order synchronization with Shopify Admin.
Content Engine Sanity Headless CMS
Editorial Control: Dedicated Sanity Studio environment allowing non-technical staff to update hero banners, subtext, and promotional grids instantly.
Visual Editing: Real-time visual previews of content changes directly on the storefront before publishing.
Backend & Operations Shopify Admin API
Source of Truth: Complete inventory management, order fulfillment, shipping integrations, and customer data tracking.
PAM Auto Components AI Assistant Custom AI Chatbot
Smart Chatbot: A floating AI assistant integrated directly into the storefront, capable of answering technical auto parts questions and return policy queries using a vector knowledge base.
